How to Play


Welcome to Better Clicker Game! Here's how to play:
  1. Click on the yellow button beside the Banana, each time you click it you'll gain $1. Additionally, the white bar will begin to fill up, and when it reaches the end you'll level up! Each time you level up you produce more money per click.
  2. Once an item reaches max level, it will begin automatically generating coins, and the progress bar will stay filled.
  3. On the left side there is a shop where you can buy special items to help you!
